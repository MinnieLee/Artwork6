Channels of Information

Information

  • Content: Channels of Information
  • Size: 33.5 x 46 cm (21 sec)
  • Year: 2020

    • Description

  • Information creates value. While working for the school newspaper, I had chances to interview and report on many social issues. Doing the activity, I learned how powerful media, channels of information, could be. This art work depicts different kinds of media that have provided people with information. Its message is that regardless of types of media, information reigns our life as a vital source of human life. To depict the vitality of information, I have created this work into two formats: one as printed work and the other as a video.
